Call Preflight

Preflight your video call

Run a quick preflight check before your next meeting — test your camera and microphone, see yourself on screen, and hear your voice with a realistic 2-second delay, exactly how your call partner would experience you.

What you can check

  • Camera preview — Switch between available webcams and verify framing, lighting, and background.
  • Microphone check — Hear yourself through a WebRTC loopback with 2-second delay, simulating real call latency.
  • Device memory — Your selected camera, mic, and theme are saved locally in your browser.
  • Private by design — No accounts, no analytics, no server-side processing. Everything runs in your browser.

How it works

Call Preflight establishes a WebRTC connection with itself, routing your camera and microphone through the same path a real video call would use. Audio and video are delayed by two seconds so you can catch awkward pauses, background noise, or lighting issues before you join.
