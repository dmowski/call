Contact & Feedback

We'd love to hear from you. Call Prep is an open tool built to help people feel confident before video calls. Your feedback helps us improve.

How to reach us

Send feedback, bug reports, or feature requests via email:

feedback@call-prep.app

What to include

  • A brief description of your feedback or issue
  • Your browser and operating system (e.g. Chrome on macOS)
  • Steps to reproduce, if reporting a bug

We read every message. Because Call Prep runs entirely in your browser with no accounts, we cannot look up session data — please include any relevant details in your email.
